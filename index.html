    What does a hero really need?

    Such a huge selection! How can I last longer in battle? What ability will bring my opponents to their knees? Does the Divine Rapier fit my boots? Even the greatest heroes need mentors to help them learn the basics of strategy through guides and hero builds. Now, in the Guides section of the Steam Community, you can view carefully crafted walkthroughs for your favorite heroes. In addition, you can subscribe to item assemblies that, right in the game, will give you advice on what (and when) abilities to learn and what items will best suit you in a given situation. As for the heroes you already know like the back of your hand, you can create your own builds for them, and then share them with your friends and the Dota 2 community.
